  |Pricing & Project Milestones
At Tasteology, we believe in transparency, clarity, and mutual success. That's why we've developed a pricing and milestone system that gives you full visibility into your investment and our process.

Starting at $5,000
Our product development projects begin at $5,000, which covers the initial concept formulation phase. This starting price may vary based on product complexity, category-specific needs, or additional services like regulatory reviews or clinical support. Every quote is custom, tailored to your product goals, timelines, and technical requirements.

1. Discovery & Scope
We align on your product vision, technical requirements, target market, and commercialization goals.

2. Concept Formulation (Starting at $5,000)
We develop one or more benchtop formulations using targeted ingredients, with consideration for functionality, cost, and label requirements.

3. Sensory Testing
We conduct in-house or panel-based sensory evaluations to assess taste, aroma, texture, and overall consumer appeal. This helps inform iteration decisions and consumer readiness.

4. Iteration & Optimization
We refine the formulation based on your feedback and sensory results, optimizing for taste, stability, and manufacturability.

5. Scale-Up Support
We assist with pilot trials, connect you to manufacturing partners, and help you prepare for commercial production.

6. Final Documentation
We deliver a complete technical dossier, including final formulation, supplier info, processing parameters, and support documents.

Each stage is quoted transparently, with no hidden fees beyond the agreed-upon scope.

Who are your clients?
▼
We partner with food & beverage startups, established CPG brands, chefs, and founders who want to develop high-quality, clean-label, commercially viable products.
Where do you operate?

We are headquartered in Miami, Florida, USA, and serve food and beverage brands globally—including the United States, Middle East (including Beirut), Europe, and beyond—through remote consultations and regional lab partnerships.

Yes, we can support the full development process, including brand and packaging guidance (depending on the project).
